Sipping vs. Chugging

Drink water slowly, small sips at a time. Gulping large volumes floods the kidneys, triggering rapid urination, while sipping allows cellular osmosis and optimal hydration.

ALKALIZING

Cucumber Rosewater

Promotes cellular cooling & hydration.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup organic cucumber slices
  • 1 tbsp organic rosewater
  • 1 liter pure alkaline water

Preparation Instructions

  1. Thinly slice the cucumber and place it inside a glass carafe.
  2. Add rosewater, then pour in 1 liter of fresh alkaline water.
  3. Infuse in the refrigerator for 2-4 hours before serving over ice.
